About Origin Driving School

Established in 2015, Origin Driving School provides professional driving lessons across the inner city and surrounding suburbs. All instructors are fully qualified and hold Working With Children checks, medical driving checks, and police clearances. We aim to help learners pass their VicRoads driving tests and gain confidence on the road.

  • Professional Training

    Learn with qualified instructors using dual-control vehicles.

  • Flexible Scheduling

    Book lessons online and manage your progress easily.
